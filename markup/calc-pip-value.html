Know it better — Value of a pip

Although knowing the actual value of a pip in the U.S. dollars is trivial for such currency pairs as EUR/USD, GBP/USD, it is quite hard to tell the pip value for these currency pairs if your account is denominated in other currencies, or for any other pairs, which have a base currency other than your account is.

These currency pairs also require the knowledge of the bid/ask price to convert the pip value to the account currency.
